Help Shape STEM Education in Doña Ana County

The Doña Ana Community STEM Survey helps us understand the real needs of families, students, teachers, and community members across the region. Your input guides our programs in coding, robotics, digital literacy, and more.

Whether you're a parent, student, educator, or just someone who cares about local access to technology and education — your voice matters.

The survey takes about 5 minutes and is available in English and Spanish.
