Support Roadrunner STEM Initiative

Why Your Support Matters

Doña Ana County faces significant challenges in digital equity and STEM education access. Your support helps us deliver programs that build essential skills for today's digital world.

With your help, we can:

  • Provide low-cost STEM workshops for underserved students
  • Offer digital literacy training to families and seniors
  • Develop comprehensive curriculum for all ages
  • Acquire technology and supplies for hands-on learning
  • Reach communities with limited access to digital resources

Support Our Mission

Our greatest supporters are the people of Doña Ana County. From parents and teachers to local leaders and lifelong learners, this initiative is built by and for our community.

We're also proud to collaborate with local partners like Las Cruces Public Schools (LCPS) and New Mexico State University (NMSU), who help amplify our impact through space, expertise, and outreach.

Whether you're able to contribute your time, resources, or platform, your support helps us expand access to hands-on STEM experiences that can change lives.

Ways You Can Help

  • Make a one-time or recurring donation
  • Sponsor a student or camp
  • Offer your space or services for workshops
  • Become a volunteer or guest speaker
  • Share our mission with your network
Donate or Sponsor

Ways to Give

Make a Donation

Your financial contribution directly supports our programs and operations. Every donation—no matter the size—helps us close the digital divide in our community.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Local businesses can support specific programs, sponsor events, or provide in-kind donations. Sponsorship opportunities start at affordable levels to accommodate businesses of all sizes.

Sponsorship Benefits:

  • Recognition on our website and program materials
  • Community goodwill and visibility
  • Direct impact on local digital literacy
  • Tax benefits (pending 501(c)(3) approval)

Volunteer

Share your time and talents with our community! We need volunteers for:

  • Workshop assistance
  • Technology setup and support
  • Event coordination
  • Administrative support
  • Community outreach

Equipment Donations

Have gently used laptops, tablets, or other technology equipment? Your donated devices can find new purpose in our programs and workshops.

Grant Connections

Know of grant opportunities that align with our mission? Help us connect with funding sources that support digital literacy and STEM education.
