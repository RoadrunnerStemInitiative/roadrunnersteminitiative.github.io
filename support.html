Support Our Mission

Our greatest supporters are the people of Doña Ana County. From parents and teachers to local leaders and lifelong learners, this initiative is built by and for our community.

We’re also proud to collaborate with local partners like Las Cruces Public Schools (LCPS) and New Mexico State University (NMSU), who help amplify our impact through space, expertise, and outreach.

Whether you're able to contribute your time, resources, or platform, your support helps us expand access to hands-on STEM experiences that can change lives.

Ways You Can Help

  • Make a one-time or recurring donation
  • Sponsor a student or camp
  • Offer your space or services for workshops
  • Become a volunteer or guest speaker
  • Share our mission with your network
