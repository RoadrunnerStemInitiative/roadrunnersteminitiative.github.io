About Us

Roadrunner STEM Initiative is a nonprofit organization committed to expanding access to digital literacy and STEM education in Doña Ana County. We aim to bridge the digital divide by providing hands-on workshops, camps, and resources for students, parents, and seniors alike.

Our programs focus on essential skills like coding, robotics, computer literacy, and cybersecurity—equipping the community to thrive in a tech-driven world. Whether it's preparing students for future careers, helping parents navigate digital tools, or empowering seniors to stay connected and secure online, our work is rooted in service and equity.

Roadrunner STEM is in its early stages and currently building strong partnerships with local educators, volunteers, and supporters. Together, we aim to launch impactful programming beginning in 2025.
