About Roadrunner STEM Initiative

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Tomas Valdez, Roadrunner STEM Initiative emerged from a clear community need. Our surveys across Doña Ana County revealed significant gaps in accessible STEM education and digital literacy resources. We discovered limited year-round options for students, few affordable programs, and a lack of technology education during school breaks.

Our Mission

We exist to close the digital divide in Doña Ana County by delivering accessible, hands-on STEM learning and digital literacy programs to underserved students, families, and communities.

Our Vision

A Doña Ana County where all residents—regardless of economic status, location, or background—have the digital literacy skills needed to thrive in today's technology-driven world.

Our Approach

Roadrunner STEM Initiative takes a phased, community-centered approach:

  1. Phase 1: Middle school workshops in coding, robotics, and digital literacy
  2. Phase 2: Expanded programs for elementary and high school students
  3. Phase 3: Comprehensive digital literacy for adult learners and families

We believe in making technology education:

  • Accessible - Low-cost or free programs
  • Practical - Skills that translate to real-world applications
  • Inclusive - Designed for diverse learning needs and backgrounds
  • Engaging - Hands-on learning that inspires curiosity
