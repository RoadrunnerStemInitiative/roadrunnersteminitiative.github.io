Our Leadership

Roadrunner STEM Initiative is establishing a dedicated leadership structure to guide our mission of closing the digital divide in Doña Ana County.

Help Lead Roadrunner STEM Initiative

We're building a leadership team that reflects our commitment to digital equity and STEM access in Doña Ana County. Our Executive Board will work closely with the Executive Director to launch and grow programs that serve students, families, and the broader community.

Organizational Structure

Our organization is built on a foundation of collaborative leadership with clearly defined roles to ensure accountability and effective implementation of our programs.

Founder & Executive Director

Tomas Valdez

As founder, Tomas brings vision and direction to the Roadrunner STEM Initiative, drawing on extensive experience in technology education and community development.

Board Member Responsibilities

  • Support the development and oversight of STEM programming strategy
  • Advocate for our mission in the community and build partnerships
  • Assist with fundraising, grant opportunities, and sustainability planning
  • Attend quarterly board meetings (virtual or hybrid)
  • Collaborate with the Executive Director and volunteers to guide organizational growth

Board Positions We're Recruiting

  • Board Chair: Leads meetings, sets agendas, and facilitates board engagement.
  • Vice Chair: Supports the chair and acts in their absence.
  • Secretary: Maintains records and documents board decisions.
  • Treasurer: Oversees the budget, finances, and reporting.
  • Executive Director: (Tomas Valdez) Carries out the board's vision and manages day-to-day operations.
  • General Members: Serve as ambassadors, lend expertise (e.g., education, technology, fundraising), and help shape direction.

How We Work Together

Roadrunner STEM Initiative is led by the Executive Director, who works closely with the board to ensure programs meet the needs of our community. This is a partnership—board members help set direction, solve challenges, and expand our impact. Together, we're building something meaningful.

Our Team

As we grow, we'll build a team of dedicated professionals and volunteers including:

  • Contract educators providing specialized instruction
  • Program volunteers supporting workshops and events
  • Administrative support staff
  • Community ambassadors promoting our mission

If you're passionate about STEM, education, and community impact—join us in shaping the future of digital access in Southern New Mexico.
